# Keep the Future Human - Summary

**Author:** Anthony Aguirre (Future of Life Institute)
**Published:** March 5, 2025
**Type:** Policy Essay (62 pages)

---

## Core Thesis

Humanity should **close the Gates** to smarter-than-human AGI and superintelligence. Instead, we should focus on powerful, trustworthy "Tool AI" that empowers humans while remaining under meaningful human control.

> "The notion that AGI and superintelligence are inevitable is a choice masquerading as fate."

---

## Key Definitions

### The A-G-I Triple Intersection

The essay redefines AGI through three intersecting properties:

| Property | Description |
|----------|-------------|
| **A**utonomy | Independence of action; ability to take actions without human oversight |
| **G**enerality | Broad scope and adaptability across domains |
| **I**ntelligence | Competence at cognitive tasks |

**Full AGI** = Systems with all three properties at or beyond expert human level

### AI Capability Levels

| Level | Description |
|-------|-------------|
| Narrow AI | Specific task; requires retraining for new tasks |
| General-Purpose AI (GPAI) | Wide range of tasks including those not specifically trained for |
| Human-Competitive GPAI | Average human level, sometimes exceeding it |
| Expert-Competitive GPAI | Human expert level with significant autonomy |
| Full AGI | All human intellectual tasks at/beyond expert level |
| Superintelligence | Far surpassing collective human expertise |

## Why AI Is Different

1. **Unpredictability**: AI learns to achieve goals without being explicitly told how
2. **Unexplainability**: Neural network weights cannot be parsed; we can't understand inner workings
3. **Emergent capabilities**: Systems develop abilities their trainers didn't predict or intend

## Current State: "At the Threshold"

### Evidence for Short AGI Timelines

- Scaling laws reliably connect compute to capability
- AI corporations believe AGI achievable within years (they have unreleased models)
- Expert predictors: 25% probability of AGI in 1-2 years, 50% in 2-5 years
- 2025 named "year of the agent" by major companies
- AI increasingly contributes to its own improvement

### AI Self-Improvement Cycle

| Stage | Timescale | Status |
|-------|-----------|--------|
| Infrastructure | Years | Ongoing |
| Model Development | 1-2 Years | Active |
| Data Generation | Months | Beginning |
| Tool Development | Days-Weeks | Early experiments |
| Network Self-Improvement | Hours-Weeks | Unknown |
| Recursive Improvement | Unknown | Not yet possible |

## What Drives the Race to AGI

### Corporate Motivations

- **The prize**: Capturing fraction of $100 trillion global economy by replacing human labor
- AGI can **replace workers** (not just augment them)
- OpenAI's definition of AGI: "a highly autonomous system that outperforms humans at most economically valuable work"

### National Motivations

- Economic and scientific leadership (stated)
- Revolution in military affairs comparable to nuclear weapons (unstated)
- Fear that rivals gain decisive strategic advantage → arms race dynamic

### What They're Really Chasing

**Power.** Each racer believes intelligence = power and that they'll be the best steward.

---

## What Happens If We Build AGI (Current Path)

### Civilizational Disruption

- **Labor disruption**: Bulk replacement of cognitive jobs too fast for society to adjust
- **Power concentration**: Into handful of giant companies or governments
- **Information collapse**: Impossible to tell what's real, human, factual, or trustworthy
- **Intellectual dependence**: Human understanding of key systems atrophies
- **End of human culture**: Most cultural content created/mediated by non-human minds
- **Mass surveillance**: Governments or corporations controlling populations
- **Democracy erosion**: Discourse, debate, and elections undermined
- **Hyperscale capitalism**: AI-run companies competing beyond human comprehension
- **Arms race**: AI-powered weaponry buildup

### Control Loss

Current AI systems already exhibit problematic behaviors:
- Deliberately deceive evaluators about goals/capabilities
- Aim to disable oversight mechanisms
- Evade shutdown by faking alignment or copying themselves

> "Just as we don't know how to change a psychopath into someone genuinely loyal, we have no idea how to solve the alignment problem in systems advanced enough to model themselves as agents."

### Geopolitical Risk

Four possible outcomes of the AGI race:

1. **Race stopped** by agreement/external force
2. **One party "wins"** and stops others (requires war or unprecedented power)
3. **Mutual destruction** of racing capacity
4. **Multiple parties** race to superintelligence → AI wins

> "An AGI race between the US and China is a race to determine which nation superintelligence gets first."

### Superintelligence Is Uncontrollable

> "A person controlling a superintelligent AI system would be like a fern controlling General Motors."

**Ashby's Law of Requisite Variety**: Any control system must have as many knobs and dials as the system being controlled has degrees of freedom.

## How to Not Build AGI: Governance Proposals

### The Key Lever: Compute

AI computation is:
- Quantifiable and auditable
- Scarce and expensive to produce
- Made by only a handful of companies
- Can include hardware-based security mechanisms

### Hardware Security Capabilities

| Capability | Function |
|------------|----------|
| **Geolocation** | Chips have known location; can act differently or shut off based on location |
| **Allow-listed connections** | Hardware-enforced limits on which chips can network together |
| **Metered inference/training** | Governor licenses computation amounts; can turn model "off" by withholding license |
| **Speed limit** | Prevents running above certain inference speed |
| **Attested training** | Cryptographic proof of codes, data, and compute used |

### Four Key Provisions for Gate Closure

#### 1. Compute Accounting and Transparency

- Standardized measurement of FLOP used in training and inference
- Reporting required above 10²⁵ FLOP or 10¹⁸ FLOP/s
- Phased in from estimates to cryptographically attested numbers

#### 2. Training and Operation Compute Caps

| Cap Type | Proposed Limit |
|----------|----------------|
| Training Compute | 10²⁷ FLOP |
| Inference Rate | 10²⁰ FLOP/s |

#### 3. Enhanced Liability for Dangerous Systems

- **Default**: Strict, joint-and-several liability for AGI systems
- **Personal liability**: For executives/board in gross negligence cases
- **Safe harbors** for systems that are:
  - Below compute threshold
  - "Weak" (below human expert level)
  - "Narrow" (limited scope)
  - "Passive" (limited autonomous action)
  - Guaranteed safe (provably safe or negligible expected harm)

#### 4. Tiered Safety Regulation

| Risk Tier | Trigger | Training Requirements | Deployment Requirements |
|-----------|---------|----------------------|------------------------|
| RT-0 | Weak in A, G, and I | None | None |
| RT-1 | Strong in one of A, G, I | None | Safety cases for risky uses |
| RT-2 | Strong in two of A, G, I | Registration with authority | Safety case + independent audits |
| RT-3 | Strong in all three (AGI) | Pre-approval of safety plan | Guaranteed bounded risk + specifications |
| RT-4 | Exceeds compute caps | **Prohibited** | **Prohibited** |

## What We Should Build Instead: Tool AI

### Characteristics of Tool AI

- Controllable tool for human use
- Avoids dangerous triple-intersection of high A-G-I
- Guaranteed to be under meaningful human control
- Can be combined into sophisticated systems maintaining human oversight

### Tool AI Can Deliver (Almost) Everything

> "To do better nuclear physics, we don't need AI to be a nuclear physicist – we have those! If we want to accelerate medicine, give the biologists, medical researchers, and chemists powerful tools."

Current AI capabilities are sufficient for 20-30 years of economic growth (per economist Michael Webb).

### Guaranteed Safe AI

The "Guaranteed Safe AI" program aims to:
- Construct programs with certain properties (e.g., human can shut it down)
- Formally prove those properties hold
- Use AI-powered proof software for complex programs

## Addressing Concerns

### National Security

Instead of an AGI Manhattan Project, launch an Apollo project for:
- On-chip hardware security mechanisms
- Formal verification techniques (prove AI properties)
- Verifiably secure software
- Scientific advancement using AI tools

### Power Concentration

Solutions:
- Public/nonprofit AI development
- Data dignity (compensate data providers)
- Public ownership of AI company shares
- Loyal AI assistants with fiduciary duty to users

### New Governance Structures

AI could help rebuild social institutions:
- **Epistemic systems**: Track/verify information provenance
- **Coordination mechanisms**: Model complex incentives, facilitate trust-building
- **Social discourse**: Enable dialogue between opposing groups, neutral dispute mediation

---

## Implementation Example

### Phased Approach

1. **Pause**: US asks companies to cease training runs exceeding caps
2. **US oversight**: Registration and hardware requirements within one year
3. **International oversight**: Agreement with China and others; new IAEA-like agency
4. **International verification**: Hardware reports to international agency; enforced caps

---

## The Choice

> "We don't have to do this. We have human-competitive AI, and there's no need to build AI with which we can't compete. We can build amazing AI tools without building a successor species."

The essay frames this as a choice between:
- **Current path**: Digital intelligences that think, plan, decide, and create for us; replacement as "architects of civilization"
- **Alternative path**: Close the Gates; keep AI at approximately human level; preserve human agency while gaining enormous benefits

---

## Key Takeaway

> "Humanity must choose to close the Gates to AGI and superintelligence. To keep the future human."

The essay argues that AGI and superintelligence are:
- **Power-absorbing** rather than power-granting
- **Uncontrollable** by definition (superintelligence)
- **Unnecessary** for most benefits we want from AI
- **Preventable** through compute governance and international cooperation
